 Simple Lease Agreement

This Lease Agreement is executed on the 1st day of October, 2000, between A, identified by the Ethereum address 0x..., as the Lessor, and B, identified by the Ethereum address 0x..., as the Lessee, for the rental of the following item:

- Product Name: thing
- Daily Rent: 100 currency units
- Rental Period: 30 days

Lease Terms:
- Start Date: The lease commences on the 5th day of October, 2000.
- End Date: The lease concludes on the 5th day of November, 2000.
- Payment Time: The initial payment is due on the Start Date.
- Payment Interval: Rent is payable every 10 days thereafter.
- Regular Payment: The amount for regular rent payments is 1,000 currency units.
- Extension Daily Rent: The daily rent for lease extension is 200 currency units.
- Security Deposit: A security deposit of 500 currency units is required.
- Contract Execution Date: This Agreement is entered into on the 1st day of October, 2000.

Clauses:

1. Initial Payment and Security Deposit: Prior to the Start Date, the Lessee, B, shall pay to the Lessor, A, the first payment for the rent plus the Security Deposit.

2. Regular Rent Payments: Commencing from the Start Date, the Lessee shall make rent payments every ten days up to and including the End Date.

3. Lease Extension: Prior to the End Date, the Lessee may input the number of days for lease extension and make the corresponding Extension Payment (this process may be repeated).

4. Lessor's Decision on Extension: Following the Lessee's request for extension as per Clause 3, the Lessor shall decide whether to agree to the extension (this process may be repeated).
   - If the Lessor agrees to the extension, the End Date shall be amended accordingly.
   - If the Lessor does not agree to the extension, the Extension Payment shall be refunded to the Lessee.

5. Contract Termination and Refund of Security Deposit: After the End Date, the Lessor shall conclude the contract, return the Security Deposit to the Lessee, and this Agreement shall be deemed complete.

Default and Termination Terms: In the event of any breach of contract terms by either party, the defaulting party shall be subject to penalties as stipulated herein and shall compensate the non-breaching party for any losses incurred.

Governing Law and Dispute Resolution: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where the Lessor, A, is located. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved amicably, or failing that, by arbitration in accordance with the rules of the arbitration institution agreed upon by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Simple Lease Agreement on the date first written above.

Signed by:

- Lessor: A (Ethereum Signature)
- Lessee: B (Ethereum Signature)
