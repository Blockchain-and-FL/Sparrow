 Standard Lease Agreement 
（Based on Simple Lease Agreement plus Property Rights and Breach Provisions）

This Lease Agreement is executed on the 1st day of October, 2000, between A, identified by the Ethereum address 0x..., as the Lessor, and B, identified by the Ethereum address 0x..., as the Lessee, for the rental of the following item:

- Product Name: thing
- Daily Rent: 100 currency units
- Rental Period: 30 days
- Ownership: The Lessor, A
- Usage Right: The Lessor, A (transferred to the Lessee during the rental period)

Lease Terms:
- Start Date: The lease commences on the 5th day of October, 2000.
- End Date: The lease concludes on the 5th day of November, 2000.
- Payment Interval: Rent is payable every 10 days.
- Regular Payment: The amount for regular rent payments is 1,000 currency units.
- Extension Daily Rent: The daily rent for lease extension is 200 currency units.
- Security Deposit: A security deposit of 500 currency units is required.
- Maximum Delay in Delivery/Payment: 5 days.
- Daily Penalty for Delay in Delivery/Payment: 20% of the daily rent.
- Contract Execution Date: This Agreement is entered into on the 1st day of October, 2000.

Clauses:

1. Initial Payment: Prior to the Start Date, the Lessee, B, shall pay to the Lessor, A, the first payment for the rent.

2. Transfer of Usage Right: Following the payment in Clause 1, the Lessor shall transfer the usage right of the item to the Lessee before the Start Date.

3. Delayed Transfer of Usage Right with Penalty: If the transfer of usage right in Clause 2 is not completed within five days after the Start Date, the Lessor shall pay a daily penalty and transfer the usage right.

4. Breach for Failure to Transfer: If the usage right transfer and payment in Clauses 2 and 3 are not completed more than five days after the Start Date, the Lessor shall pay a daily penalty, return the Security Deposit, and terminate this Agreement.

5. Regular Rent Payments: Commencing from the Start Date, the Lessee shall make rent payments every ten days up to and including the End Date.

6. Late Payment with Penalty: If the Lessee's payment is delayed but does not exceed five days, the Lessee shall pay a penalty in addition to the payment.

7. Breach for Late Payment: If the Lessee's payment is delayed for more than five days, the Lessor may repossess the property, and this Agreement shall be terminated.

8. Lease Extension: Prior to the End Date, the Lessee may input the number of days for lease extension and make the corresponding Extension Payment (this process may be repeated).

9. Lessor's Decision on Extension: Following the Lessee's request for extension as per Clause 8, the Lessor shall decide whether to agree to the extension (this process may be repeated).
   - If the Lessor agrees to the extension, the End Date shall be amended accordingly.
   - If the Lessor does not agree to the extension, the Extension Payment shall be refunded to the Lessee.

10. Contract Termination: After the End Date, the Lessor shall repossess the usage right of the property, return the Security Deposit, and this Agreement shall be deemed complete.

Default and Termination Terms: The terms outlined above shall govern the conduct of the lease, payment obligations, and any breaches thereof. The party at fault shall bear the corresponding legal responsibilities and compensate the non-breaching party for any losses incurred.

Governing Law and Dispute Resolution: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where the Lessor, A, is located. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved amicably, or failing that, by arbitration in accordance with the rules of the arbitration institution agreed upon by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Standard Lease Agreement on the date first written above.

Signed by:

- Lessor: A (Ethereum Signature)
- Lessee: B (Ethereum Signature)
